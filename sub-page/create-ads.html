Increase the number of page likes (fans) which would give you a bigger audience including the friends of fans. This type allows your ads to be exposed to every ad place of Facebook. Exclusive CPL(cost per click) product is available only for Adwitt users - Charge only when Facebook users become your fan.

Promote your contents to get more people to see and increase your chance of engagement. Photo, movie clips, link, offer, poll and other various contents can be advertised.

Drive traffic from Facebook to your website (external URL), targeting everyone on Facebook. If you have Facebook page, you can also get the traffic from news feed of desktop and mobile. Once you set the ‘Conversion tracking’ code, data of lead, registration, add to cart, purchase and ROAS can be tracked.

Get more people to install and use your Facebook app or native mobile app by targeting the people who are more likely to install. Social feature supporting by Facebook will help your message to be recognized as friends’ voice.
